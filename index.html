The Best Burgers in Chicago.

    THE RANKINGS

  1. The Loyalist
      What makes it so good?
    • Caramelized onion mayo
    • House made poppy seed bun

  2. Red Hot Ranch
      What makes it so good?
    • Old School Chicago Classic
    • $6 gets you a double and fries
    • Smashed to perfection, but eat it fast!

  3. Au Cheval
      What makes it so good?
    • The gold standard for Chicago burgers
    • Prime beef from W.W. Johnson Farms
    • Griddle is kissed by sizzling bacon before grilling

  4. Portillo's
      What makes it so good?
    • Chicago's iconic fast food join serves up nostalgia
    • Charbroiled for a smokey undertone
    • Served with crinkle fries and a chocolate cake shake

  5. MOMOTARO
      What makes it so good?
    • The Momo Royal served on a house made bao bun
    • Accompanied by satsumaimo fries
    • Ancient Secret? Miso Mayo(shhh)
